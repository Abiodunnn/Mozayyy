PROBLEM STATEMENT

With new films coming out and cutting-edge technology, the movie business is always changing. However, users may find it difficult to locate and reserve movie tickets, which makes for a bad user experience. Exodus is creating a movie showtime app to streamline this procedure and give users a smooth experience.

 

In order to make it simple for users to find the movies they want to watch, check showtimes, reserve tickets, and use other functions of the app, AS A UI/UX designer I need to create an intuitive and visually appealing interface. The app should be created to accommodate both frequent and infrequent moviegoers, ensuring that everyone has a good time.

GOAL STATEMENT
My objective is to develop a smooth and simple user interface that will make it simple for moviegoers to look up and choose showtimes, buy tickets, and schedule their visits to the theater. I want to create an interface that is visually appealing, engages and delights people, and gives them the knowledge they require to make wise decisions. Additionally, I work hard to incorporate user input and insights at every stage of the design process to enhance the usability, functionality, and overall user experience of the app. My ultimate objective is to develop a unique showtime app that gives moviegoers a fun and stress-free moviegoing experience.

USER RESEARCH SUMMARY

The Research are base on people who go to the movies at least twice a month. I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to better understand the users' needs.

Because of their hectic schedules, the primary users identified were young working adults and full-time students who did not have time to wait in line for tickets and snacks.

The research confirms assumptions about the challenges that cinemagoers face, but it also reveals that long lines are not the only challenges they face at the cinema. Other issues that moviegoers face include seat availabilty and a lack of movie trailers on booking apps.

What I Learnt with this design project
As a UI/UX designer, the movie showtime app project gave me invaluable insights and lessons. I can keep producing outstanding user experiences if I apply these lessons to my upcoming projects.
